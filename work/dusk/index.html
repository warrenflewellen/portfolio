Work About
Verde Solar

Project Type: Branding

Role: Graphic Designer

Year: 2025

Overview

A conceptual rebrand for Verde Solar, a new player in the renewable energy sector. The company specializes in high-grade solar panels and needed an identity that felt bold, modern, and trustworthy. This project explores how visual branding can help a fresh face cut through a saturated market.

Challenge

Verde Solar wanted to establish itself as the bold new kid on the block in an industry dominated by traditional, established brands.


The company needed to communicate:
  • Approachability and transparency
  • A forward-thinking mission
  • A clean, professional look
  • Broad appeal to consumers and businesses

Solution

A brand identity system that reflects optimism, innovation, and sustainability.


Built through:
  • Bold typography
  • Vibrant color palette
  • Custom logo that balances solar symbolism with modern design
  • Applications that reinforce a consistent, human-centered voice

Deliverables

The Verde Solar brand system was applied across a range of digital and print materials to ensure consistency and impact. Each application reinforces the brand's clean, modern aesthetic while adapting to different formats and use cases.
