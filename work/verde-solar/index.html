Work About
Verde Solar

Project Type: Branding

Role: Graphic Designer

Year: 2025

Overview

Verde Solar is a renewable energy company offering high-grade solar panels. The project focused on creating a bold, modern identity that inspires trust.

Challenge

Stand out in a traditional, competitive industry while appealing to both consumers and businesses.

Solution

A clean, vibrant brand identity system with custom logo, typography, and color palette applied across digital and print materials.

Deliverables

Brand visuals and applications reinforcing Verde Solar’s modern, approachable, and forward-thinking image.
