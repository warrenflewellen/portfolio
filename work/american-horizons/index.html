Editorial + Book Design

American Horizons

American Horizons is a conceptual book design that captures the breadth of America’s national parks, from Yosemite’s granite walls to Death Valley’s shifting sands. GT America was chosen for its boldness in capturing the American spirit, paired with Univers for clarity, allowing full-color imagery and captions to present the parks as both living heritage and shared legacy.

  • Role Graphic Designer
  • date 2025

01challenge

The challenge was to design a book that showcased the vast diversity of America’s national parks without overwhelming the viewer with text or imagery. It needed to feel informative yet visually immersive.

02solution

A clean layout with GT America and Univers typefaces balanced clarity with impact, allowing full-bleed photography to take center stage. Concise captions and short chapter texts supported the visuals, creating a design that feels modern, authoritative, and timeless.
